Cafe Medina

Today I went to Cafe Medina.This place I heard a lot good things from my friends, and I am finally free to try it out. Usually I called to check for the waiting time, lucky there wasnt any wait today.

I really like the interior design, the light looks fabulous, simple yet unique. Beside the light, the wood pattern of the tables looks crazy.

Fricasse is on the breakfast and it is served all day. It comes with 2 fried eggs, braised short ribs, roasted potatoes, baslamic onion, watercress, applewood chedder, grilled foccacia. The taste is very powerful and I was thinking it will be too heavy and strong for breakfast. Overall I like it.

I was surprised with the waffle and caramel topping. When I first looked at the caramel topping, it looks light and watery. However after dip the waffle into the caramel, the thickness of the topping is just right. I love the waffle. Now I want another one :P

***Cafe Medina opens Monday- Friday 8:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m., Weekends 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.

  • Breakfast: 9:00 a.m.- Noon
  • Lunch: Noon - 3:00 p.m.
  • Website: http://www.medinacafe.com/

Cafe Medina is always busy and they do not take reservations.
