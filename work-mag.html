Food Magazine Layout Design Project

TOP 5 Desserts

Year /Time Take

2013/ 4 Days

Tools

Adobe Indesign

Background

I came up with this Top5 desserts project idea. I thought it would be interesting to find out the most popular desserts in the Lower Mainland. I interviewed 30 people and asked the same question " What is your favourite dessert?". The result is ice cream, waffle, bubble tea, macaroon and shaved Ice. I would like to present the result with magazine layout.

I attempted to show the ranking by a podium, 1st - 5th; however I found that black podium will take so much space. Therefore I changed it to an invisible podium. Through placing each dessert in each column and different height, I would like to show higher the dessert image is, the higher the ranking.
